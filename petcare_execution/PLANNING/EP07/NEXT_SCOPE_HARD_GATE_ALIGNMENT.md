# NEXT_SCOPE_HARD_GATE_ALIGNMENT

## Planning Phase
PETCARE-PHASE-1-NEXT-SCOPE-PLANNING-AFTER-EP06

## Selected Epic
EP-07 B2B Marketplace Integration

## Hard Gate Alignment

### G-C1 Clinical Safety Gate
Wave impact:
Indirect in early waves, stronger in later partner referral and service publication flows.

Planning rule:
EP-07 must not alter clinical sign-off, escalation, or medication safety semantics.

### G-A1 AI Governance Gate
Wave impact:
Future assistive recommendation use only.

Planning rule:
No autonomous partner selection, pricing approval, or settlement decisioning.

### G-R1 Regulatory and Privacy Gate
Wave impact:
High

Planning rule:
Partner verification, transaction eligibility, and partner data handling must remain auditable and compliant.

### G-S1 Security Gate
Wave impact:
High

Planning rule:
Partner role enforcement, access control, and audit emission are mandatory from WAVE-01.

### G-O1 Operational Readiness Gate
Wave impact:
High

Planning rule:
SLA representation, breach visibility, and partner operational state must be introduced in staged governed waves.

## Gate-Relevant Focus For WAVE-01
- partner onboarding record completeness
- verification transition controls
- audit event readiness
- role-restricted onboarding actions
- deterministic query surfaces

## Gate-Relevant Focus For Later Waves
- contract and SLA enforcement
- pricing rule auditability
- settlement reconciliation traceability
- partner scorecard evidence readiness

## Conclusion
EP-07 is gate-compatible and ready to start with WAVE-01 as long as the onboarding registry is implemented as a deterministic, auditable, non-autonomous foundation.
