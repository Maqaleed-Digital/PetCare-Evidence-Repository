OPERATIONS AI BOUNDARY

Purpose:
Preserve assistive-only AI behavior in platform operations runtime.

Allowed:
- partner review summary drafting
- operational risk flag surfacing
- structured recommendation support
- scorecard commentary drafting

Not allowed:
- autonomous partner approval
- autonomous SLA exception approval
- autonomous pricing override
- autonomous settlement finalization

Human authority:
- platform and clinic administrators retain final authority
- all AI outputs require human review before governed action
