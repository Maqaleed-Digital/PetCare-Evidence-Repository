# PETCARE — EP-12 HARD GATES

## G-F21 AI Advisory Only
- AI outputs remain advisory only
- no execution, approval, rejection, dispatch, or finalization authority
- advisory boundary explicitly documented

## G-F22 Explainability Required
- every recommendation provides visible rationale
- explanation references signal or rule basis
- unexplained recommendations are not allowed

## G-F23 Recommendation Traceability
- recommendation id, source, timestamp, and subject recorded
- operator acceptance or rejection captured where applicable
- provenance preserved in audit evidence

## G-F24 No Silent Influence
- AI suggestions visibly surfaced
- no hidden queue reorder
- no hidden score impact on execution outcome
- operator aware when AI influenced a view or suggestion

## G-F25 Human Override Always Required
- operator may ignore, reject, or override AI suggestion
- override path remains available
- no workflow forces AI recommendation acceptance

## Pass Conditions
- tests pass
- evidence pack generated
- manifest generated
- working tree committed and pushed
