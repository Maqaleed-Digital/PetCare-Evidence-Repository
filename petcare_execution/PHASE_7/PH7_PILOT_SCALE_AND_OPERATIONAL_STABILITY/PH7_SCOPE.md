PETCARE-PHASE-7 — PILOT SCALE + OPERATIONAL STABILITY

OBJECTIVE
Scale the controlled PetCare pilot from first-workflow readiness into a governed multi-participant operating state with stability monitoring, incident handling, SLA visibility, and repeatable evidence generation.

PILOT SCALE TARGET
- 3 to 5 clinics maximum
- multiple real veterinarians
- controlled real owners
- governed role-safe access only
- no uncontrolled public expansion

OPERATIONAL FOCUS
- repeated workflow execution across pilot participants
- incident tracking
- issue categorization
- response ownership
- SLA behavior visibility
- workflow completion monitoring
- audit evidence continuity

IN SCOPE

1. Pilot Scale Control
- clinic participation cap
- participant tracking
- pilot cohort governance
- rollout boundary definition

2. Operational Stability
- incident register
- severity model
- response owner tracking
- workflow success and failure logging
- operational observations

3. Service Monitoring
- route availability checks
- sign-in path visibility
- unauthorized behavior visibility
- workflow execution evidence continuity

4. Governance Continuity
- no bypass of UI
- no weakening of RBAC
- no hidden execution
- no sign-off bypass
- fail-closed remains active

NON-NEGOTIABLES
- no fake usage
- no demo or prototype accounts as pilot proof
- no DB shortcuts
- no hidden execution
- no uncontrolled scale beyond pilot cap
- no weakening of clinical sign-off control

SUCCESS CONDITION
PH7 proves that PetCare can sustain a controlled pilot across multiple clinics and users with stable route behavior, governed workflows, incident visibility, and auditable operational evidence.
