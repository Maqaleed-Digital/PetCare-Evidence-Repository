Material Disclosure and Truth Boundary Standard

Purpose

Define the rules for disclosing material constraints, open gaps, and conditional posture in external readouts.

Disclosure Rules

MD-01
Conditional readiness must be stated as conditional when relevant

MD-02
Open gaps must not be hidden if they materially affect readiness, scale, certification, or risk

MD-03
Controlled production status must not be presented as unrestricted public scale

MD-04
Documentary-only or pending-validation controls must not be presented as runtime-validated controls

MD-05
All external summaries must preserve truth consistency with the audit readiness baseline

Universal Rule

No external readout may sacrifice material truth for narrative strength.
