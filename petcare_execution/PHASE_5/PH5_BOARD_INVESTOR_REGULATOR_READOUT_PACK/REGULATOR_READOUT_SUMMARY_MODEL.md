Regulator Readout Summary Model

Purpose

Define the governed structure for presenting PetCare to regulatory or compliance review audiences.

Required Sections

1. Constitutional control baseline
2. Human authority and assistive-only AI boundary
3. Runtime enforcement and fail-closed posture
4. Controlled production activation model
5. Evidence and audit readiness posture
6. Open limitations, exclusions, and pending validations
7. Review traceability references

Universal Rule

Regulator summaries must remain conservative, explicit, and evidence-linked.
