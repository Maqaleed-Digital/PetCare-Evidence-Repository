Certification Posture Declaration Model

Purpose

Define the allowed certification readiness outcomes for PetCare.

Allowed Postures

CP-01
not_ready
Use when required evidence, mappings, or review basis are materially incomplete

CP-02
conditionally_ready
Use when major governance and evidence baselines exist but open gaps or pending validations remain

CP-03
readiness_established
Use only when evidence, scope, traceability, and review basis are sufficient for formal assessment intake

Universal Rule

The certification posture must be conservative, evidence-backed, and explicitly approved.
