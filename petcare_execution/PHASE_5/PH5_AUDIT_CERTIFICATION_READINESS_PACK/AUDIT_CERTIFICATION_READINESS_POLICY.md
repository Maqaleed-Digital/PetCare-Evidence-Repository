Audit and Certification Readiness Policy

Policy Objective

Ensure PetCare can be reviewed under a formal audit or certification process using governed evidence, explicit scope, transparent gaps, and accurate readiness claims.

Policy Rules

AR-01
Every readiness activation must have an audit readiness owner, approval reference, audit scope reference, certification posture reference, and review mode.

AR-02
Every readiness review must identify:
1. in-scope governance layers
2. in-scope runtime controls
3. in-scope evidence packs
4. in-scope operational procedures
5. open exclusions or limitations

AR-03
Every reviewed control must be classified as:
1. evidence_ready
2. conditionally_ready
3. documentary_only
4. pending_validation
5. gap_open

AR-04
No control may be represented as audit-ready unless the linked evidence source, traceability source, and review status are explicit.

AR-05
All open gaps, documentary-only controls, and pending validations must be recorded in the readiness gap register.

AR-06
Certification posture must reflect actual evidence and review state, not target ambition.

AR-07
No readiness pack may conceal unresolved findings, incomplete mappings, or pending validations.

Minimum Evidence per Readiness Action

1. readiness_id
2. owner
3. approval reference
4. audit scope reference
5. certification posture
6. reviewed control count
7. open gap count
8. review timestamp
9. audit trace id
