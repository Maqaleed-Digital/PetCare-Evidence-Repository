Operational Expansion and Contraction Model

Purpose

Define accountable operation during expansion and the governed path for rollback, pause, or contraction if scale conditions degrade.

Operational Roles

OC-01
Scale owner

OC-02
Incident owner

OC-03
Hypercare owner

OC-04
Escalation path owner

OC-05
Review cadence owner

Contraction Requirements

CT-01
Contraction trigger classes declared

CT-02
Contraction authority path declared

CT-03
Restoration baseline declared

CT-04
Contraction evidence required

CT-05
Post-contraction review required

Universal Rule

No controlled scale expansion may operate without named owners and executable contraction posture.
