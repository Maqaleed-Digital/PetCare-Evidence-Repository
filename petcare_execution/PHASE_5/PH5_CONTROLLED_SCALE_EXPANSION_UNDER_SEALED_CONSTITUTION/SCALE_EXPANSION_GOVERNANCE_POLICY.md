Controlled Scale Expansion Governance Policy

Policy Objective

Ensure PetCare expands only through governed, reviewable, and reversible scale activation under the sealed constitution.

Policy Rules

SE-01
Every scale expansion activation must have a scale expansion owner, approval reference, expansion scope reference, scale readiness standard reference, and review mode.

SE-02
Every scale activation must declare its expansion class:
1. clinic_expansion
2. partner_expansion
3. region_expansion
4. service_expansion
5. limited_scale_pilot

SE-03
Every activation must identify the affected operating scope, runtime control posture, operational ownership, and rollback posture.

SE-04
No scale activation may proceed while runtime guardrails, fairness protections, dispute controls, or operational ownership are undefined or overloaded.

SE-05
Any materially adverse expansion condition must trigger block, controlled pause, fallback, or rollback according to approved recovery rules.

SE-06
No expansion may weaken constitution-locked invariants, audit posture, or truth-bound external representations.

SE-07
Every expansion must preserve named ownership for:
1. scale owner
2. incident owner
3. hypercare owner
4. escalation owner
5. review cadence owner

Minimum Evidence per Scale Activation

1. scale_activation_id
2. owner
3. approval reference
4. expansion class
5. expansion scope reference
6. readiness standard reference
7. operational capacity reference
8. rollback posture
9. review window
10. audit trace id
