# PARTNER NETWORK SCALING GOVERNANCE

## Purpose
This document governs external service and partner scaling around a multi-clinic PetCare network.

## Scope
Partner categories covered by this governance model:

- pharmacy and medication fulfillment partners
- diagnostics and laboratory partners
- referral and emergency partners

## Governance rules
1. Partner participation must not weaken the approved clinical approval chain.
2. External partner integrations must preserve event traceability.
3. Site-level partner usage must remain visible at portfolio level.
4. Incident routing must remain attributable by clinic and partner type.
5. No partner dependency may become a hidden safety bottleneck.

## Admission controls for partner scaling
Before a partner is allowed into a wave:

- service category must be assigned
- scope of use must be defined
- clinic applicability must be defined
- escalation owner must be defined
- fallback path must be defined

## Control tiers
### Tier 1
Core operational partners needed for clinic continuity.

### Tier 2
Extended operational partners used by some clinics.

### Tier 3
Strategic scale partners used only after proven steady-state performance.

## Required reporting
Partner governance must support:

- partner usage by clinic
- incident count by partner category
- fallback activation frequency
- dependency concentration view
