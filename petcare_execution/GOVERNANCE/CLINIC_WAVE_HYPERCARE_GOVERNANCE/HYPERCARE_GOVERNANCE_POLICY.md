# HYPERCARE GOVERNANCE POLICY

## Purpose
This document governs the supervised hypercare period immediately following controlled activation of new clinic-wave sites.

## Hypercare principle
Hypercare is a temporary but mandatory heightened-governance operating period. A newly activated clinic is not treated as ordinary steady-state until hypercare exit is explicitly approved.

## Governance posture
The approved posture is:

1. supervised early operations
2. evidence-rich observation
3. rapid issue escalation
4. explicit remediation handling
5. portfolio-level exit authority

## Deterministic clinic placeholders
This pack preserves governance-safe identifiers only:

- CLINIC-WAVE2-001
- CLINIC-WAVE2-002
- CLINIC-WAVEN-001
- CLINIC-WAVEN-002

## Hypercare objectives
Hypercare must verify:

- first-day controls remain effective
- assistive AI boundary remains preserved
- override and escalation behavior remains controlled
- reporting completeness is stable
- partner fallback behavior is visible
- no material unresolved safety issue exists

## Hard stop posture
Any material issue discovered in hypercare may require:

- extended hypercare
- targeted remediation
- pause of clinic progression toward steady-state

## Output of this pack
This pack defines the governance model for hypercare but does not assert that any real clinic has already completed hypercare.
