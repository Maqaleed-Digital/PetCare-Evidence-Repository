# HYPERCARE_DEVIATION_AND_REMEDIATION_RULES

## Purpose
This document defines how deviations discovered during hypercare are classified and handled.

## Deviation categories
Deviations are classified as:

1. reporting deviation
2. staffing deviation
3. operational deviation
4. AI safety deviation
5. escalation deviation
6. partner dependency deviation

## Required remediation responses
A detected deviation may require one of the following responses:

- monitor_within_hypercare
- targeted_remediation
- extend_hypercare
- block_steady_state_exit

## Rule
No unresolved material deviation may be ignored or closed without explicit governance review.

## Escalation rule
Material deviations must be surfaced to the command model established in prior packs.
