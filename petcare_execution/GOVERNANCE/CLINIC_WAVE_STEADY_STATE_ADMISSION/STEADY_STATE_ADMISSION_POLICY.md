# STEADY STATE ADMISSION POLICY

## Purpose
This document governs admission of clinic-wave sites from hypercare into steady-state portfolio operation.

## Admission principle
Steady-state admission is an explicit governance decision. Completion of activation or hypercare alone does not automatically grant steady-state status.

## Governance posture
The approved posture is:

1. steady-state admission only after successful hypercare review
2. portfolio-level authority retained
3. evidence-backed admission decisions
4. continued assistive AI boundary preservation
5. ongoing portfolio comparison after admission

## Deterministic clinic placeholders
This pack preserves governance-safe identifiers only:

- CLINIC-WAVE2-001
- CLINIC-WAVE2-002
- CLINIC-WAVEN-001
- CLINIC-WAVEN-002

## Admission objectives
Steady-state admission must verify:

- hypercare has produced stable operating evidence
- no unresolved material deviation blocks admission
- reporting and KPI visibility remain complete
- escalation posture remains functional
- partner dependency posture remains controlled
- steady-state control posture is accepted

## Hard stop posture
A clinic must not be admitted to steady-state if any material issue remains unresolved.

## Output of this pack
This pack defines the governance model for steady-state admission but does not assert that any real clinic has already been admitted.
