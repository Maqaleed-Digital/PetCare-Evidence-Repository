# EXECUTIVE PORTFOLIO VISIBILITY POLICY

## Purpose
This document governs executive visibility across the steady-state PetCare multi-clinic portfolio.

## Governance principle
Executive visibility is a governed summary layer, not an operating substitute. It must preserve portfolio truth, highlight material exceptions, and support intervention without bypassing established operating controls.

## Governance posture
The approved posture is:

1. executive visibility remains portfolio-wide
2. executive summaries remain evidence-backed
3. exception visibility remains active
4. intervention attention remains explicit
5. underlying assistive AI and human approval boundaries remain unchanged

## Deterministic clinic placeholders
This pack preserves governance-safe identifiers only:

- CLINIC-LIVE-BASELINE-001
- CLINIC-WAVE2-001
- CLINIC-WAVE2-002
- CLINIC-WAVEN-001
- CLINIC-WAVEN-002

## Executive visibility objectives
The executive layer must ensure:

- steady-state posture is visible at portfolio level
- cross-clinic comparison remains understandable
- exceptions are surfaced clearly
- intervention attention is prioritized
- review cadence remains governable

## Output of this pack
This pack defines the executive visibility layer for the governed steady-state portfolio and does not alter operational decision authority.
