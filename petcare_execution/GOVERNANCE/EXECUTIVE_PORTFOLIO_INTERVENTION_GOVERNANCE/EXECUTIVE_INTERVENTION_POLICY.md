# EXECUTIVE INTERVENTION POLICY

Purpose:
Defines the governance model for executive intervention across the steady-state PetCare clinic portfolio.

Principles:
1. intervention authority is portfolio-governed
2. intervention follows escalation signals
3. intervention does not bypass operational governance
4. intervention must be recorded and auditable
5. intervention must link to remediation governance

- CLINIC-WAVE2-001
- CLINIC-WAVE2-002
