# DRIFT AND REMEDIATION REENTRY GOVERNANCE

## Purpose
This document defines the governance path when a steady-state clinic drifts from required controls or must re-enter remediation.

## Drift categories
Drift is classified as:

1. reporting drift
2. operational drift
3. AI safety drift
4. escalation drift
5. partner dependency drift
6. KPI drift

## Required governance responses
Detected drift may require one of the following responses:

- portfolio_watch
- targeted_remediation
- steady_state_reclassification
- controlled_reentry_to_remediation

## Rule
Material drift must not be ignored. Any clinic with unresolved material drift remains subject to portfolio intervention.

## Reentry rule
A clinic that re-enters remediation must do so through explicit governance classification rather than ad hoc local handling.
