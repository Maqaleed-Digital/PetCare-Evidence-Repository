# GOVERNANCE CLOSURE CRITERIA

## Purpose
This document defines the criteria required to declare portfolio governance complete.

## Closure criteria
The following criteria are mandatory:

1. live operations governance established
2. wave activation governance established
3. hypercare governance established
4. steady-state admission governance established
5. multi-clinic steady-state governance established
6. executive visibility governance established
7. executive intervention governance established
8. audit evidence chain established
9. source-of-truth continuity preserved

## Closure decision
The only allowed closure outcomes are:

- governance_complete
- governance_incomplete

## Rule
Portfolio governance may only be declared complete when all closure criteria are satisfied.
