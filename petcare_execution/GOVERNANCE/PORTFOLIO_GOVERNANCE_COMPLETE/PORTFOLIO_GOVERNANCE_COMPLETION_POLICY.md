# PORTFOLIO GOVERNANCE COMPLETION POLICY

## Purpose
This document defines the formal completion policy for PetCare portfolio governance after the full governed execution chain has been established.

## Governance completion principle
Portfolio governance completion means the PetCare operating model, clinic-wave model, steady-state portfolio model, executive visibility layer, and executive intervention layer are all defined, linked, evidence-backed, and auditable.

## Completion posture
The approved posture is:

1. governance chain completed end-to-end
2. evidence chain completed end-to-end
3. portfolio scope explicitly defined
4. executive oversight model explicitly defined
5. post-completion boundaries explicitly defined

## Portfolio scope
This completion pack preserves governance-safe placeholders only:

- CLINIC-LIVE-BASELINE-001

## Rule
Governance completion does not mean product evolution stops. It means the governed portfolio operating baseline is formally complete.
