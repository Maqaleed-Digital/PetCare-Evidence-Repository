# POST COMPLETION BOUNDARY AND NEXT PHASE

## Purpose
This document defines the operating boundary after governance completion and the correct interpretation of what comes next.

## Post-completion boundary
After governance completion:

- the governance baseline is considered formally complete
- future work must build as controlled extensions
- no future phase may rewrite closure history
- future execution must reference the completed governance baseline

## Next phase interpretation
The next phase is not more governance closure. The next phase is controlled enhancement, product expansion, or operating optimization branching from a completed governance foundation.

## Allowed next-phase categories
- controlled product enhancement
- controlled operational optimization
- controlled portfolio scaling
- controlled reporting refinement
