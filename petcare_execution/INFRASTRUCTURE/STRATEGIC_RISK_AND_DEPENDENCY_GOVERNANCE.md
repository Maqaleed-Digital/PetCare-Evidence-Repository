PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
STRATEGIC_RISK_AND_DEPENDENCY_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the governance model for strategic risks and dependencies affecting live PetCare operations and scale.

Risk Record Fields
- risk_id
- category
- description
- owner
- likelihood
- impact
- mitigation
- dependency_reference
- review_status
- next_review_date

Dependency Categories
- internal platform dependency
- vendor dependency
- partner dependency
- clinic expansion dependency
- regulatory dependency

Rules
- open strategic risks require owners
- unresolved high-impact dependencies must remain visible in executive review
- mitigation plans require named actions and review dates
