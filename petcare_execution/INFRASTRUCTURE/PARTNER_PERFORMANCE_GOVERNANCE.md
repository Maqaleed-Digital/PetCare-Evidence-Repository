PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
PARTNER_PERFORMANCE_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the executive governance model for partner and vendor performance in live operations.

Performance Dimensions
- SLA adherence
- incident contribution
- delivery responsiveness
- compliance or audit exceptions
- dependency risk
- improvement responsiveness

Required Outputs
- partner status
- key issues
- owner
- corrective action
- next review date

Rules
- underperforming partners require corrective action owners
- critical partner risks escalate into executive review
- partner status must remain evidence-backed
