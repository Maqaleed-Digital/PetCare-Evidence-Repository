NATIONAL_REFERRAL_LOAD_BALANCING_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the governance model for referral and load balancing across the national network.

Balancing Scope
- clinic referral distribution
- overflow handling
- emergency redirection visibility
- partner overflow utilization
- regional balancing decisions
- escalation ownership

Required Outputs
- current balancing status
- overflow hotspots
- balancing actions
- owner
- next review date

Rules
- referral balancing must remain auditable
- severe overflow hotspots require immediate owner assignment
- unmanaged referral imbalance must escalate into national command review
