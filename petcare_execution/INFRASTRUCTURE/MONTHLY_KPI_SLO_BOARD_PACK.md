PETCARE-STEADY-STATE-OPERATIONS-AND-CONTINUOUS-IMPROVEMENT-GOVERNANCE
MONTHLY_KPI_SLO_BOARD_PACK

Purpose
Define the monthly board-facing KPI and SLO pack for live PetCare operations.

Minimum Monthly Pack
- availability trend
- incident trend by severity
- mean time to acknowledge trend
- mean time to mitigate trend
- audit path continuity trend
- AI governance path continuity trend
- deployment success trend
- release calendar summary
- top risks and mitigations
- top improvement items

Rules
- metrics must be trend-based, not snapshot-only
- threshold breaches must be explicitly highlighted
- board pack must reference evidence sources
- carry-forward risks must remain visible until closed
