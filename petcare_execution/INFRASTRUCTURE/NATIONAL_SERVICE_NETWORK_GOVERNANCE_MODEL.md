NATIONAL_SERVICE_NETWORK_GOVERNANCE_MODEL

Purpose
Define the governance model for PetCare as a national service network with multiple regional operating units.

State Transition
- Current: petcare_multi_clinic_scale_execution_governed
- Target: petcare_national_service_network_governed

Scope
- national service governance
- regional operating governance
- cross-region service consistency
- national partner network oversight
- inter-clinic escalation and referral control
- national capacity balancing
- regional resilience governance
- scale-to-network transition control

Named Roles
- national service owner
- regional operating owner
- national partner network owner
- network governance and risk owner
- clinical operations escalation owner
- executive network approver

Rules
- regional ownership must remain named
- national service standards must remain visible across regions
- unresolved cross-region blockers prevent network maturity declaration
- inter-clinic escalation paths must remain governed and auditable
- network decisions must remain evidence-backed

Success Rule
National service network governance is active only when ownership, operating controls, and regional governance packs are documented and linked to evidence.
