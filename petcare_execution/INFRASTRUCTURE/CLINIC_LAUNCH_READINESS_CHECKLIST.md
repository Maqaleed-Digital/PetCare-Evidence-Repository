PETCARE-SCALE-EXPANSION-READINESS-AND-MULTI-CLINIC-EXECUTION-GOVERNANCE
CLINIC_LAUNCH_READINESS_CHECKLIST

Purpose
Define the readiness checklist required before launching a new clinic or operating node.

Readiness Areas
- staffing and operating ownership ready
- runtime and access ready
- observability ready
- audit path ready
- AI governance path ready
- partner and supplier onboarding ready
- support and incident readiness ready
- training and operating procedure readiness ready

Decision Outputs
- ready
- conditionally_ready
- not_ready

Rules
- readiness must be explicit per clinic
- conditionally_ready requires named conditions and owners
- not_ready blocks launch
