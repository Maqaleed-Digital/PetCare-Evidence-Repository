Purpose
Define the weekly service review required for governed steady-state operations.

Required Review Sections
- review_week
- service owner
- current production release
- health summary
- incident summary
- problem summary
- KPI and SLO trend summary
- audit continuity summary
- AI governance continuity summary

- decisions and owners

Rules
- weekly review must occur on a named cadence
- unresolved critical issues must appear in review
- all decisions require named owners
- review evidence must be retained
