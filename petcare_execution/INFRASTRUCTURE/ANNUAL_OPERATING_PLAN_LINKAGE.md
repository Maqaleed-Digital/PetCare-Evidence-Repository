PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
ANNUAL_OPERATING_PLAN_LINKAGE

Purpose
Define how live service governance links into the annual operating plan.

Required Linkage Areas
- service reliability objectives
- risk reduction objectives
- scale and clinic expansion objectives
- partner performance objectives
- investment priorities
- staffing and support considerations
- governance and compliance objectives

Rules
- quarterly operating review outputs must feed annual planning
- open strategic risks must be visible in planning linkage
- major investments must reference service evidence and operating priorities
