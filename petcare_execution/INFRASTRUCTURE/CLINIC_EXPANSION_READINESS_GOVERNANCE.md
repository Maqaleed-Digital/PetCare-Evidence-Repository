PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
CLINIC_EXPANSION_READINESS_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the governance criteria for expanding PetCare service coverage across additional clinics or operating nodes.

Readiness Dimensions
- operational stability
- staffing readiness
- runtime and capacity readiness
- observability readiness
- audit and AI governance continuity
- partner and clinic onboarding readiness
- support model readiness

Decision Outputs
- ready
- conditionally_ready
- not_ready

Rules
- expansion readiness must be evidence-backed
- conditionally_ready requires named conditions and owners
- not_ready items require remediation path before approval
