PETCARE-NATIONAL-SERVICE-NETWORK-AND-REGIONAL-OPERATING-GOVERNANCE
NATIONAL_CAPACITY_BALANCING_MODEL

Purpose
Define the model for balancing service capacity across the national network.

Capacity Dimensions
- regional runtime capacity
- staffing and support capacity
- clinic throughput capacity
- partner service capacity
- referral overflow capacity
- observability and operations capacity

Required Outputs
- current capacity distribution
- imbalance risks
- balancing actions
- owner
- review date

Rules
- capacity imbalance risks require named actions
- severe imbalance must appear in national review
- balancing assumptions must be evidence-backed
