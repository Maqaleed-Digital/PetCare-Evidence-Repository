VENDOR_AND_PARTNER_OPERATIONS_REVIEW

Purpose
Define the periodic operations review for external vendors and partners.

Review Scope
- integration health
- SLA adherence
- incident contribution
- unresolved risks
- compliance or audit exceptions
- improvement actions
- owner and due date

Rules
- external dependency issues must remain visible
- partner-caused blockers require named follow-up
- critical external risks must escalate into weekly review
