PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
QUARTERLY_OPERATING_REVIEW_PACK

Purpose
Define the quarterly operating review pack for PetCare executive governance.

Required Sections
- quarter
- current source-of-truth commit
- service health summary
- KPI and SLO trend summary
- incident and problem trend summary
- audit and AI governance continuity summary
- clinic expansion status
- partner performance status
- strategic risks and dependencies
- investment priorities
- decisions and owners

Rules
- quarterly review must be trend-based
- prior quarter carry-forward risks must remain visible
- every decision requires named owners
- evidence references are mandatory
