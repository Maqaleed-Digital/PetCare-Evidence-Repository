CROSS_REGION_SERVICE_CONSISTENCY_CONTROLS

Purpose
Define the controls required to keep service delivery consistent across regions.

Consistency Dimensions
- operating procedures
- incident handling
- audit continuity
- AI governance continuity
- release and change discipline
- partner onboarding standards
- escalation handling
- reporting cadence

Control Outputs
- compliant
- conditionally_compliant
- intervention_required

Rules
- cross-region variances must be visible
- intervention-required regions need owners and deadlines
- critical control breaks must escalate into national review
