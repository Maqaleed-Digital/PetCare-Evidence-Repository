PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
SERVICE_PORTFOLIO_HEALTH_MODEL

Purpose
Define the health model used to evaluate PetCare as a live service portfolio.

Portfolio Health Dimensions
- service reliability
- clinical and pharmacy operations stability
- audit continuity
- AI governance continuity
- partner service health
- growth readiness
- change and release discipline
- improvement execution

Health Outputs
- healthy
- watch
- intervention_required
- executive_attention_required

Rules
- health status must be evidence-based
- intervention-required items require owners
- executive-attention items must appear in quarterly review
