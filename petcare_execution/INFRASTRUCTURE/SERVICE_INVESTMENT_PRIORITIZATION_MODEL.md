PETCARE-EXECUTIVE-OPERATING-SYSTEM-AND-PORTFOLIO-SERVICE-GOVERNANCE
SERVICE_INVESTMENT_PRIORITIZATION_MODEL

Purpose
Define the prioritization model for service investments in live PetCare operations.

Prioritization Factors
- operational risk reduction
- clinical and pharmacy service impact
- reliability uplift
- governance and audit strength
- scale enablement
- partner and clinic readiness impact
- delivery effort
- dependency complexity

Priority Outputs
- immediate
- next_cycle
- backlog
- defer

Rules
- prioritization must remain evidence-based
- immediate priorities require named owners
- deferred items require rationale
