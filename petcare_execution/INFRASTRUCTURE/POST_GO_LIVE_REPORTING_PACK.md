PETCARE-PRODUCTION-HYPERCARE-AND-OPERATIONS-GOVERNANCE
POST_GO_LIVE_REPORTING_PACK

Purpose
Define the reporting pack required during and at the end of hypercare.

Required Reports
- daily operational review snapshots
- incident summary by severity
- KPI and SLO trend summary
- deployment and change log summary
- audit continuity summary
- AI governance continuity summary
- clinic and partner operational exceptions
- open actions and owners

Rule
Post-go-live reporting is incomplete unless daily reviews and incident summaries are linked to evidence.
