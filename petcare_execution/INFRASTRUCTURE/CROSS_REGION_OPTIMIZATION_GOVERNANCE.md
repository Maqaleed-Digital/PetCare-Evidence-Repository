Purpose
Define the governance model for optimization across regions in the national network.

Optimization Dimensions
- service utilization balancing
- clinic throughput balancing
- support load balancing
- partner routing optimization
- change and release coordination
- capacity efficiency improvement

Outputs
- optimized
- optimization_in_progress
- intervention_required

Rules
- optimization decisions must be evidence-backed
- intervention-required items need owners and dates
- cross-region optimization gaps must remain visible in national review
