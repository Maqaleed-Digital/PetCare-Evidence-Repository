POST_EXPANSION_STABILIZATION_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the stabilization governance required after each expansion wave.

Stabilization Scope
- live health verification
- incident trend review
- audit continuity review
- AI governance continuity review
- partner issue review
- support load review
- open actions and owners

Rules
- every wave must enter a named stabilization window
- blocking incidents prevent wave closeout
- stabilization evidence must be linked before wave completion
