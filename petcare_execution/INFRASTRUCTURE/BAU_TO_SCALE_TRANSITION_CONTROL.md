Purpose

- steady-state governance active
- executive governance model active
- quarterly operating review pack active
- strategic risk and dependency governance active

- investment prioritization active

- final evidence manifest generated

Target State

Rule
BAU-to-scale transition is complete only when all transition criteria are satisfied and linked to evidence.
