PETCARE-ENTERPRISE-NETWORK-OPTIMIZATION-AND-NATIONAL-ORCHESTRATION-GOVERNANCE
EXPANSION_OPTIMIZATION_BACKLOG_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the backlog governance model for post-scale optimization opportunities.

Backlog Fields
- optimization_id
- source
- linked region or network area
- owner
- priority
- expected benefit
- target review cycle
- status

Rules
- optimization items must have owners
- high-priority items must appear in national optimization review
- closed items require outcome confirmation
- deferred items require rationale
