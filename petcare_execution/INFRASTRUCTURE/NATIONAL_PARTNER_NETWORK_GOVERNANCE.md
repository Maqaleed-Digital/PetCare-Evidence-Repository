NATIONAL_PARTNER_NETWORK_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the governance model for the national partner network across regions.

Network Dimensions
- SLA adherence
- readiness by region
- integration consistency
- compliance and audit standing
- support and escalation responsiveness
- concentration risk

Required Outputs
- partner network status
- region coverage summary
- open partner risks
- owner
- next review date

Rules
- critical partner risks must remain visible nationally
- cross-region partner inconsistency requires remediation ownership
- no national partner expansion without readiness evidence
