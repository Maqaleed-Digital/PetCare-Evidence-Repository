PETCARE-PRODUCTION-HYPERCARE-AND-OPERATIONS-GOVERNANCE
HYPERCARE_CLOSEOUT_CRITERIA

Purpose
Define the criteria required to close hypercare and transition to steady-state governed operations.

Mandatory Closeout Criteria
- hypercare operating window completed
- no open Sev-1 incidents
- no unowned Sev-2 incidents
- KPI and SLO reviews completed
- audit path continuity confirmed
- AI governance path continuity confirmed
- daily operational reviews completed for the active window
- change freeze exit approved
- final evidence manifest generated

Target State
petcare_hypercare_governed_operations_active

Closeout Rule
Hypercare closes only when all mandatory closeout criteria are satisfied and linked to evidence.
