PETCARE-SCALE-EXPANSION-READINESS-AND-MULTI-CLINIC-EXECUTION-GOVERNANCE
REGIONAL_PARTNER_ONBOARDING_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define governance for onboarding regional partners during expansion.

Onboarding Scope
- operational readiness
- SLA readiness
- integration readiness
- compliance and audit readiness
- escalation readiness
- support ownership

Required Outputs
- partner status
- owner
- readiness result
- open conditions
- next review date

Rules
- no regional partner goes live without readiness result
- open conditions require owners
- critical partner blockers must appear in wave review
