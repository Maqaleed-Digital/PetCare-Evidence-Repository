INTER_CLINIC_ESCALATION_AND_REFERRAL_GOVERNANCE

Purpose
Define the governance model for inter-clinic escalation and referral across the PetCare network.

Governance Scope
- escalation trigger ownership
- referral routing ownership
- emergency coordination visibility
- pre-handoff information continuity
- audit traceability
- post-event follow-up ownership

Rules
- escalation and referral paths must be auditable
- emergency referral blockers must escalate into regional review
- inter-clinic handoff continuity must remain visible
- no uncontrolled bypass of governed referral path
