PH6.4 WORKFLOW VALIDATION CHECKLIST

W-01 APPOINTMENT
- owner authenticated
- owner enters permitted route
- appointment action initiated through UI
- appointment result recorded
- audit evidence captured

W-02 CONSULTATION
- vet authenticated
- vet enters permitted route
- consultation opened through UI
- consultation result recorded
- audit evidence captured

W-03 SIGN-OFF
- consultation sign-off performed by vet
- sign-off remains human-controlled
- sign-off result recorded
- audit evidence captured

W-04 PRESCRIPTION OR CONTROLLED OUTCOME
- prescription or controlled clinical outcome recorded through permitted workflow
- no hidden execution
- result recorded
- audit evidence captured

W-05 GOVERNANCE OVERSIGHT
- admin role available for governance oversight if needed
- wrong-role access not observed
- any governance exception recorded

MANDATORY EVIDENCE
- owner participant recorded
- vet participant recorded
- admin participant recorded
- appointment outcome recorded
- consultation outcome recorded
- sign-off outcome recorded
- prescription or controlled outcome recorded
- notes on any mismatch or stop condition

STOP CONDITIONS
- wrong-role access observed
- sign-off bypassed
- demo or prototype account used as pilot proof
- direct DB shortcut used
- missing evidence for critical step
