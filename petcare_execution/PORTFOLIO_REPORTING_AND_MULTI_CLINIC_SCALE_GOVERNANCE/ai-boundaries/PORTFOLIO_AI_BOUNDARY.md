PORTFOLIO AI BOUNDARY

Purpose:
Preserve assistive-only AI behavior during portfolio reporting and multi-clinic scale governance.

Allowed:
- summarization
- draft analysis
- KPI commentary drafting
- variance clustering
- governance commentary drafting

Not allowed:
- autonomous executive decision
- autonomous intervention approval
- autonomous reporting override
- autonomous governance override

Human authority:
- reporting, intervention, and governance decisions remain human-approved actions
