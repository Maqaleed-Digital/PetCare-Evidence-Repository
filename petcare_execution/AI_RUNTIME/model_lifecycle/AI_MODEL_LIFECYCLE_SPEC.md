PetCare — PETCARE-AI-OPS-5
Long-Term Model Governance and Lifecycle Management

Purpose

Define lifecycle governance rules for AI models used by the PetCare platform.

Scope

• lifecycle state machine
• promotion and demotion rules
• retirement and supersession governance
• review cadence
• lifecycle evidence export

Safety Guarantees

• AI remains assistive-only
• veterinarian authority preserved
• governance council oversight required

Determinism Rules

• no randomness
• no external network calls
• deterministic governance evaluation
