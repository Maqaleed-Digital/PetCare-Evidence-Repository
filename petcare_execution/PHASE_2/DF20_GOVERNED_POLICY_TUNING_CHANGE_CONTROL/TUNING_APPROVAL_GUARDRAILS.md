# PETCARE DF20 — Tuning Approval Guardrails

## Purpose
Define hard boundaries for governed policy tuning.

## Guardrails
- no tuning without evidence from DF19
- no tuning without explicit approval
- no tuning based on hidden logic
- no autonomous learning
- no immediate production activation without recorded review

## Evidence Basis
Policy tuning must reference:
- outcome_validation_record
- feedback_record
- variance_report
- review_requirement_snapshot

## Scope Guardrail
Tuning may be scoped to:
- global
- portfolio segment
- named unit

## Time Guardrail
Temporary tuning must include an expiry or review date.
