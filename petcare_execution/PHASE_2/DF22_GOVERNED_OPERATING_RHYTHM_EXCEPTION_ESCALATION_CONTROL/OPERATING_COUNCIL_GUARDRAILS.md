# PETCARE DF22 — Operating Council Guardrails

## Purpose
Define governance boundaries for steady-state review and exception disposition.

## Guardrails
- every operating rhythm review must have an owner
- every high or critical exception must be surfaced to governance
- no policy change may occur through operating rhythm review alone
- no release promotion may occur through exception handling alone
- exception disposition must remain within approved authority boundaries

## Review Basis
Steady-state review may reference:
- DF19 feedback evidence
- DF20 policy change evidence
- DF21 ratified baseline evidence
- active operating metrics and checkpoint records

## Scope
Operating rhythm may be scoped to:
- global
- portfolio segment
- named unit
