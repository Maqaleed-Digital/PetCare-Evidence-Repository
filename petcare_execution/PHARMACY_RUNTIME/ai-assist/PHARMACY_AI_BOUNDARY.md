PHARMACY AI BOUNDARY

Purpose:
Preserve assistive-only AI behavior in the pharmacy runtime.

Allowed:
- draft safety summary
- warning surfacing
- structured review support
- risk flag prioritization support

Not allowed:
- autonomous prescription approval
- autonomous dispensing authorization
- autonomous safety override
- autonomous recall closure

Human authority:
- veterinarian and pharmacy operator retain final regulated authority
- all AI outputs require human review before regulated action
