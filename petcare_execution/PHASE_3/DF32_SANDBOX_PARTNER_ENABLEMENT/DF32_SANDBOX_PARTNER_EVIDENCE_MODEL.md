DF32 — SANDBOX PARTNER EVIDENCE MODEL

REQUIRED RECORD FIELDS

- sandbox_output_id
- partner_reference
- declared_scope
- sandbox_state
- isolation_reference
- data_scope_reference
- approval_requirement
- evidence_references
- validator_result
- timestamp

RULES

- every sandbox output must remain non-production
- every blocked output must still produce evidence
- every sandbox output must identify isolation reference
- every sandbox output must identify approval requirement explicitly
