OWNER AI INTAKE BOUNDARY

Purpose:
Preserve assistive-only AI behavior in the owner experience runtime.

Allowed:
- symptom intake drafting
- uncertainty surfacing
- red flag highlighting
- structured pre-consultation intake support

Not allowed:
- autonomous diagnosis
- autonomous prescription recommendation
- autonomous emergency finalization
- autonomous clinical decision

Human authority:
- veterinarian retains final clinical authority
- all intake outputs require vet review before clinical action
