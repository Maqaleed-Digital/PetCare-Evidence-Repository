# PH-V3 Operational Readiness Pack Scope

Phase
PH-V3

Purpose
Verify that PetCare is operationally ready to enter final go-live validation after completing construction, runtime verification, and integration verification.

Operational Readiness Focus
- clinical safety readiness
- AI governance readiness
- regulatory and privacy readiness
- security readiness
- operational monitoring and incident readiness
- go-live evidence readiness
- no-operational-drift confirmation

In Scope
- runtime layers PH-R1 through PH-R7
- verification layers PH-V1 and PH-V2
- gate obligations G-C1, G-A1, G-R1, G-S1, G-O1
- evidence-backed operational readiness assertions

Out of Scope
- new implementation
- UI mutation
- deployment mutation
- environment mutation

Exit Condition
PH-V3 is complete when operational readiness artifacts, evidence inventory, and manifest are committed and pushed cleanly.
