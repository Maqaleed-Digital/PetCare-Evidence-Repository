DF44 Platform Constitution

Constitutional Identity

PetCare is a fully governed, evidence-backed, fail-closed veterinary ecosystem platform with assistive-only AI and human final authority.

Constitutional Commitments

1. no autonomous execution
2. no coercive governance
3. no hidden economic or commercial bias
4. no unfair partner suppression
5. no retaliatory dispute handling
6. no silent runtime bypass
7. no silent post-seal mutation
8. reversibility required where operationally possible
9. appeal path required where materially adverse outcomes may occur
10. evidence chain required for governed activation, review, remediation, runtime control, and seal state

Constitutional Boundary

Any future change that weakens these commitments requires formal re-governance, new evidence, and explicit accountable approval.
