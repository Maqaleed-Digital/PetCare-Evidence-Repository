DF42 Prohibited Cross-Layer Loophole Patterns

PCL-01
Silent precedence inversion
Any undeclared reversal of which control governs a combined scenario

PCL-02
Layer chaining bypass
Any sequence of individually valid controls that together bypass a stronger governance protection

PCL-03
Exception propagation drift
Any exception that spreads beyond its approved scope into unrelated domains

PCL-04
Commercial override of trust or fairness
Any economic or commercial rule that weakens trust or fairness protections without explicit approved basis

PCL-05
Dispute-based suppression loophole
Any attempt to use dispute handling to avoid fairness or economic controls

PCL-06
Ambiguity activation
Any activation while cross-layer conflict or precedence remains unresolved

Blocking Rule

If any prohibited cross-layer loophole pattern is detected, activation must fail closed pending human review.
