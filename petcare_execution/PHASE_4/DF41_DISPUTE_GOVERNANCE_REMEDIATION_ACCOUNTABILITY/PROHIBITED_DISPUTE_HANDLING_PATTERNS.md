DF41 Prohibited Dispute Handling Patterns

PDP-01
Silent dispute suppression
Any failure to formally register, classify, or preserve a valid dispute within the governed process

PDP-02
Retaliatory treatment
Any penalty, restriction, visibility reduction, routing disadvantage, or economic pressure applied because a dispute was raised

PDP-03
Opaque resolution
Any dispute closure lacking accountable reviewer identity, rationale, and evidence link

PDP-04
Undeclared blame assignment
Any attempt to assign fault, causation, or responsibility outside approved review and documentation

PDP-05
Evidence erosion
Any deletion, hiding, or non-preservation of evidence relevant to the dispute lifecycle

PDP-06
Appeal denial by omission
Any in-scope dispute process that lacks declared appeal or escalation path

PDP-07
Non-transparent remediation
Any corrective action imposed without owner, due date, evidence requirement, or closure review

PDP-08
Automatic adverse outcome
Any autonomous refund, reversal, de-prioritization, suspension, or commercial disadvantage triggered without human review

PDP-09
Commercial-clinical crossover through dispute handling
Any use of dispute governance to alter clinical triage, emergency escalation, or medication safety logic for commercial reasons

PDP-10
Irreversible contested closure
Any disputed outcome that cannot be reviewed, appealed, or restored through an approved path

Blocking Rule

If any prohibited dispute-handling pattern is detected, activation must fail closed and remain blocked pending human review.
