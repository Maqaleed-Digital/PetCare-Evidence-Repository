DF39 Prohibited Incentive Mechanisms

PIM-01
Hidden ranking manipulation
Any undisclosed boost or suppression affecting marketplace visibility, routing, ranking, or discoverability

PIM-02
Coercive exclusivity loop
Any economic structure that materially pressures a partner into exclusivity without approved governance review

PIM-03
Opaque loyalty trap
Any mechanism that accumulates economic dependency without clear terms, expiry, and reversibility

PIM-04
Automatic pricing optimization
Any autonomous economic adjustment performed without explicit human approval and audit evidence

PIM-05
Silent revenue-share drift
Any share alteration that occurs outside documented approvals, contract references, or declared validity windows

PIM-06
Privilege escalation by tier plus incentive
Any combination of trust tier and economic incentive that produces unjustified advantage beyond approved rules

PIM-07
Clinical-path bias through commercial signal
Any commercial signal that directly or indirectly alters clinical triage, consultation authority, emergency escalation, or medication safety behavior

PIM-08
Irreversible subsidy lock-in
Any incentive that cannot be suspended or rolled back through an approved operational path

PIM-09
Shadow discounting
Any discount, rebate, or credit not visible to approved audit and commercial controls

PIM-10
Discriminatory access incentive
Any incentive structure that unfairly excludes similarly situated partners without approved and documented rationale

Blocking Rule

If any prohibited mechanism is detected, activation must fail closed and the incentive remains blocked.
