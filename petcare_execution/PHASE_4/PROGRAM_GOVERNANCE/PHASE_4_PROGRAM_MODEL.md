PHASE 4 PROGRAM MODEL — TRUSTED ECOSYSTEM SCALE + MARKET DOMINANCE LAYER

PURPOSE

Define the authoritative execution model for Phase 4 as a governed scale and market dominance program built on top of the completed Phase 3 ecosystem activation sequence.

PHASE 4 OBJECTIVE

Move from:
- governed ecosystem activation and multi-partner scale readiness

To:
- trusted ecosystem scale with governed trust expansion, controlled market leverage, evidence-backed dominance mechanics, and cumulative sovereign control

PHASE 4 IDENTITY

Phase 4 is the trust-and-scale phase in which PetCare evolves from a governed ecosystem into a trusted ecosystem infrastructure with defensible market dominance mechanics.

NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES

- no infra mutation in this execution pack
- no IAM mutation in this execution pack
- no DNS mutation in this execution pack
- no autonomous execution
- no uncontrolled commercial leverage
- no uncited trust scoring
- no hidden network dependency creation
- no local, federated, portfolio, ecosystem, or activation governance bypass
- no policy drift
- every market-facing posture must be evidence-backed
- every scale mechanism must remain approval-bound
- every dominance mechanic must remain reversible, explainable, and auditable
- commit remains the single source of truth

OPERATING MODEL

LAYER 1 — TRUSTED SCALE FOUNDATION
Use Phase 3 partner activation, trust posture, certification, and multi-partner control as the baseline.

LAYER 2 — MARKET STRUCTURE GOVERNANCE
Define how trust, participation quality, certification tiers, incentives, and market coordination are governed.

LAYER 3 — DOMINANCE MECHANICS CONTROL
Define how network effects, market position, partner value loops, and ecosystem preference signals are controlled without breaching governance.

LAYER 4 — CONTINUOUS MARKET GOVERNANCE
Maintain fail-closed oversight, evidence continuity, reversibility, and human approval over all scale and dominance mechanisms.

PROGRAM PRINCIPLES

1. trusted scale is not uncontrolled growth
2. market influence is not coercive authority
3. certification is not unrestricted privilege
4. network effects are not governance bypass
5. ecosystem strength cannot dilute sovereign control
6. every scale and dominance posture must remain explainable and evidence-linked
7. every blocked state must still generate evidence

PHASE 4 OUTCOME

Phase 4 is successful only when PetCare can scale trust, partner value, market position, and ecosystem influence without sacrificing evidence integrity, reversibility, or fail-closed governance.
