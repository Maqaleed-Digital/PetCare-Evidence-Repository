DF44 Prohibited Post-Seal Mutation Patterns

PSM-01
Silent constitution change
Any change to constitutional commitments without formal re-governance

PSM-02
Invariant weakening drift
Any weakening of the locked invariant registry without accountable approval and fresh evidence

PSM-03
Untracked seal bypass
Any operational or documentary change that treats the platform as sealed while altering the sealed baseline

PSM-04
Certification ambiguity
Any claim of final closure without explicit certification status and evidence linkage

Blocking Rule

If any prohibited post-seal mutation pattern is detected, seal activation must fail closed pending human review.
