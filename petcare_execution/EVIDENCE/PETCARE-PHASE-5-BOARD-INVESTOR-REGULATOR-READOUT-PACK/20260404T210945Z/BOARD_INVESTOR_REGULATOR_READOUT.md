PH5 — Board / Investor / Regulator Readout Pack

Purpose

Establish the formal executive and external readout layer for PetCare so that the platform's sealed governance baseline, controlled production posture, runtime traceability, and audit readiness can be communicated accurately to board, investor, and regulator audiences without overstating readiness or hiding constraints.

Authority Boundary

This pack governs board, investor, and regulator readout readiness. It does not authorize unsupported market claims, silent omission of material gaps, or any weakening of the sealed constitutional baseline for presentation convenience.

State Transition

Prior state:
AUDIT_AND_CERTIFICATION_READINESS_ESTABLISHED

Target state:
EXECUTIVE_EXTERNAL_READOUT_READINESS_ESTABLISHED

Core Principles

1. Every readout must remain evidence-backed and traceable
2. Audience-specific messaging may simplify language but may not alter truth
3. No claim may exceed the underlying evidence, runtime traceability, or readiness posture
4. Gaps, pending validations, and conditional readiness must remain visible when material
5. Board, investor, and regulator narratives must derive from the same authoritative baseline
6. No commercial storytelling may weaken constitutional, fairness, or audit truths
7. Readout artifacts must be deterministic, reviewable, and reproducible
8. AI may assist with formatting only; AI may not fabricate claims, endorsements, or readiness statements

Readout Domains

Board Governance Readout
Defines strategic governance posture, operating controls, exposure status, and decision-grade summary for board review

Investor Trust Readout
Defines institution-grade governance, risk control posture, operating readiness, and scalable trust thesis for investor diligence

Regulator Readout
Defines constitutional controls, audit readiness, runtime enforcement, evidence posture, and controlled production activation summary for regulatory review

Readout Integrity Governance
Defines approved truth boundary, material disclosure rule, and evidence linkage rule across all audiences
