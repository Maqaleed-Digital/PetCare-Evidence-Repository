Executive and External Readout Policy

Policy Objective

Ensure every board, investor, and regulator readout derives from the same governed truth baseline and preserves material accuracy.

Policy Rules

ER-01
Every readout activation must have a readout owner, approval reference, evidence baseline reference, disclosure standard reference, and audience review mode.

ER-02
Every readout must identify its audience:
1. board
2. investor
3. regulator
4. mixed executive audience

ER-03
Every readout must distinguish between:
1. sealed governance status
2. runtime implementation status
3. audit readiness status
4. conditional readiness or open gaps
5. future roadmap items

ER-04
No audience-specific summary may represent conditional readiness as complete certification or operational perfection.

ER-05
Material gaps, pending validations, documentary-only controls, and controlled production boundaries must remain visible where relevant.

ER-06
Any metric, status, or claim included in a readout must be linked to a named evidence baseline or approved reference.

ER-07
No readout may imply regulator approval, investor commitment, or board endorsement unless such approval or endorsement exists and is evidenced.

Minimum Evidence per Readout Action

1. readout_id
2. owner
3. approval reference
4. audience type
5. evidence baseline reference
6. disclosure standard reference
7. reviewed claim count
8. material gap disclosure count
9. review timestamp
10. audit trace id
