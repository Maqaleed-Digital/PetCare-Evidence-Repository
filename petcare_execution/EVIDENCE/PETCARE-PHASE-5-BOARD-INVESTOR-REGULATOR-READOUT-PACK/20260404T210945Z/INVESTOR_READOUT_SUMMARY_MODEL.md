Investor Readout Summary Model

Purpose

Define the governed structure for presenting PetCare to investors and strategic capital audiences.

Required Sections

1. Platform thesis
2. Governance moat and trust architecture
3. Controlled production posture
4. Runtime traceability and audit readiness posture
5. Risk management model
6. Scale readiness and operating roadmap
7. Material dependencies and disclosed constraints

Universal Rule

Investor summaries may communicate strategic strength but may not overstate readiness, certification, adoption, or revenue maturity.
