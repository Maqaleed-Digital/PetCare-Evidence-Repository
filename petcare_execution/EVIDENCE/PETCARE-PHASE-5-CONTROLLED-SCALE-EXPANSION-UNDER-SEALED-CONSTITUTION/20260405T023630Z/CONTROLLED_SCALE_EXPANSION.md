PH5 — Controlled Scale Expansion Pack Under the Sealed Constitution

Purpose

Establish the governed scale expansion layer for PetCare so that growth across clinics, partners, services, and operating regions occurs under the sealed constitution, controlled production posture, runtime traceability, audit readiness, and disclosure truth boundary.

Authority Boundary

This pack governs controlled scale expansion. It does not authorize uncontrolled market expansion, silent relaxation of governance, autonomous geography activation, or any weakening of constitution-locked invariants for growth convenience.

State Transition

Prior state:
EXECUTIVE_EXTERNAL_READOUT_READINESS_ESTABLISHED

Target state:
CONTROLLED_SCALE_EXPANSION_READY_UNDER_CONSTITUTION

Core Principles

1. Scale must remain subordinate to the sealed constitution
2. No new clinic, partner, region, or service expansion may proceed without accountable approval and evidence
3. Expansion must preserve runtime guardrails, fairness controls, dispute accountability, and audit traceability
4. Growth may not outpace operational ownership, hypercare, or rollback capability
5. Expansion readiness must distinguish between scalable governance design and validated runtime implementation
6. No commercial pressure may justify weakening safety, fairness, or evidence standards
7. Expansion posture must remain reversible, reviewable, and fail-closed
8. AI may assist with informational analysis only; AI may not approve scale expansion or bypass controls

Defines operational ownership, hypercare capacity, escalation bandwidth, and rollback posture for expanded scope

Fail-Closed Conditions

The system must block activation when any of the following are missing:
1. named scale expansion owner
2. approved scale expansion approval reference
3. approved expansion scope reference
4. approved scale readiness standard reference
5. declared expansion review mode

Mandatory Outputs

2. expansion scope reference
3. scale readiness standard reference
4. operational capacity reference
5. rollback and contraction reference
6. governance preservation reference
7. manifest with SHA-256

6. no policy drift from DF37 to DF44 and PH5 packs
