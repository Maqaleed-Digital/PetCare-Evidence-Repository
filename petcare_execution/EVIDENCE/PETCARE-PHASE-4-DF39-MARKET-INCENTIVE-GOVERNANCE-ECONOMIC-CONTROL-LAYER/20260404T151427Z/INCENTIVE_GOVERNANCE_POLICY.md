DF39 Incentive Governance Policy

Policy Objective

Allow only explicit, reviewable, bounded incentive mechanisms that support ecosystem growth without creating coercion, hidden bias, unfair routing, opaque monetization pressure, or uncontrolled economic optimization.

Policy Rules

IG-01
Every incentive must have an owner, approval reference, validity window, and rollback posture.

IG-02
Every incentive must map to a defined business objective:
1. acquisition
2. retention
3. service quality improvement
4. emergency coverage support
5. partner enablement
6. inventory stabilization

IG-03
Every incentive must declare the affected population:
1. all partners
2. specific approved segment
3. approved pilot cohort

IG-04
Every incentive must declare the bounded mechanism:
1. fixed subsidy
2. capped credit
3. limited-time promotional support
4. contracted revenue-share adjustment
5. performance bonus with approved metrics

IG-05
Every incentive must declare a fairness review confirming it does not create unjustified advantage across similarly situated partners.

IG-06
Every incentive must declare whether marketplace visibility, routing, ranking, or pricing is affected. If yes, explicit approval is mandatory and the effect must be visible in audit evidence.

IG-07
Incentives may inform human decisions but may not silently alter partner ranking, clinical routing, emergency prioritization, or settlement treatment.

IG-08
No incentive may conflict with clinical safety, regulatory obligations, SLA commitments, consent controls, or prior trust-tier rules.

IG-09
At expiry, suspension, or rollback, the platform must preserve historical evidence and return the system to a governed baseline.

Approval Chain

1. economic owner prepares proposal
2. fairness review confirms no unjustified advantage
3. pricing governance confirms permitted mode
4. finance or commercial approver issues approval reference
5. activation evidence pack is generated
6. monitored operation proceeds within declared validity window

Minimum Evidence per Incentive

1. unique incentive id
2. owner
3. approval reference
4. target cohort
5. objective
6. bounded mechanism
7. start date and end date
8. rollback rule
9. fairness check result
10. audit trace id
