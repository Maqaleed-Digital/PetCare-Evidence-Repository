DF40 Prohibited Market Abuse Patterns

PMA-01
Hidden partner suppression
Undeclared lowering of visibility, routing opportunity, or economic access without approved and reviewable rule basis

PMA-02
Opaque commercial steering
Any undisclosed partner preference that changes ranking, discoverability, referral flow, or routing assignment

PMA-03
Retaliatory penalty loop
Any penalty, de-prioritization, or restriction applied in response to dispute, refusal, or negotiation posture without approved rule basis

PMA-04
Silent opportunity concentration
Any mechanism that accumulates partner advantage through undeclared stacking of incentives, placement, or visibility support

PMA-05
Commercial-clinical crossover manipulation
Any use of economic pressure or commercial preference to influence clinical triage, escalation, consultation authority, or medication handling

PMA-06
Forced exclusivity pressure
Any coercive or manipulative structure that materially pressures a partner into dependency outside approved contractual governance

PMA-07
Automatic punitive enforcement
Any autonomous penalty, suspension, de-ranking, or commercial restriction executed without explicit human review and evidence

PMA-08
Appeal denial by design
Any restrictive mechanism that lacks review path, accountable owner, or appeal record

PMA-09
Discriminatory treatment without declared basis
Any materially different commercial treatment across similarly situated partners without approved and documented rationale

PMA-10
Irreversible restriction trap
Any commercial restriction that cannot be suspended, rolled back, or reviewed through an approved operational path

Blocking Rule

If any prohibited abuse pattern is detected, enforcement must fail closed and remain blocked pending human review.
