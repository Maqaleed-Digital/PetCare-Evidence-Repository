DF40 Partner Treatment Fairness Policy

Policy Objective

Ensure partner opportunity, visibility, routing exposure, commercial support, and penalty treatment remain fair, reviewable, and proportionate across similarly situated participants.

Policy Rules

PF-01
Every fairness action must have an owner, approval reference, treatment standard reference, and review mode.

PF-02
Every fairness action must declare the affected domain:
1. visibility
2. routing
3. incentive access
4. promotional support
5. restriction or penalty
6. review or appeal

PF-03
Every fairness action must define the comparison class:
1. all verified partners
2. partners by approved service category
3. partners by approved operational region
4. partners by approved readiness tier
5. partners in approved pilot cohort

PF-04
Every material difference in treatment must be justified by declared rule, capability difference, SLA difference, readiness difference, or documented compliance state.

PF-05
No fairness action may directly or indirectly alter clinical safety decisions, emergency escalation, or medication safety behavior for commercial gain.

PF-06
Any visibility, routing, or opportunity change that materially affects revenue access requires human review before activation.

PF-07
Penalty or restriction action must include reason code, evidence reference, review timestamp, and appeal path.

PF-08
Commercial fairness controls may not be used to create retaliation, coercion, forced exclusivity, or undeclared competitive suppression.

PF-09
At expiry, suspension, rollback, or appeal resolution, the platform must preserve historical evidence and restore the governed baseline defined by active approved rules.

Minimum Evidence per Fairness Action

1. fairness_action_id
2. owner
3. approval reference
4. domain affected
5. comparison class
6. rule justification
7. reason code if restrictive
8. start date and end date
9. review mode
10. rollback rule
11. appeal path
12. audit trace id
